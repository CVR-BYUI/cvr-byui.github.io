We provide comfort and convenience while you serve

Full-time Temple Missionaries receive a 20% discount when reserving a Suite. We want to provide some of the comforts of home while you're away. Suites offer a kitchenette or full kitchen, living areas to visit or just relax, and a multi-jet jacuzzi.
When you're busy, sometimes not having to drive can be a relief. Your stay includes free shuttle service to the Temple, running on the hour. There are shuttles that also visit local Church Historical sites, such as Temple Square or the Joseph Smith Farm, depending on which of our locations you stay.
